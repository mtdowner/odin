Who Let the Dogs Out!

Description

It's no secret living a life of crime typically leads to living a life inside of a cage, possibly surrounded by some scary animals. What if, however, one's only crime was that of being born? And, to top it all off, these are German Shepherds trapped with nowhere to go after being dumped on the side of a road..

Thank Doggy Heaven, though, because we are here to rectify this situation. We're on a mission to rescue these sweet puppies from the evil bars they live behind.

Now, let's get ready to break our soon-to-be friends out of doggy prison!

Ingredients

Before we become a doggy-fugitive camp, there are quite a few rules to go over and many tools we'll need. This includes:

  • A big heart
  • An even bigger yard
  • An even way bigger poopy scoopy
  • Lots of patience
  • Pen and paper
  • A strong internet connection
  • A lint roller.. or two...
  • Matching Welcome Home tees for all!

Steps

If we want to bust out our friends with few errors, research and planning is essential! Prepare yourself with these steps:

  1. Establish a secure online connection as this is a super secret mission.
  2. With your favorite search engine, search shelters in your area for German Shepherds.
  3. Make a record of each one you find.
  4. Contact each shelter to get more information about the doggy prisoners.
  5. Sort your data according to age and time served.
  6. Map out each shelter's floorplan, and when you're ready, start the mission.
  7. Ambush the shelters by setting all of the animals free.
  8. Grab the German Shepherds, making sure seniors are first out followed by those with the most time served.
  9. Book it to your car to go home!
  10. Check out your new fleet of loyal, loving, and highly-intelligent soldiers.
  11. Hand out matching tees for everyone, and celebrate the saving of so many lives!
