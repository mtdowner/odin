Become a Five-Paw Chef

Description

Take a minute to think about this question: would you like it if your dog fed you the same dry food everyday for every single meal? Studies aren't out yet, but the results would probably show a big fluffy "NOPE!"

Now, imagine how delightful it would be if dogs had their very own in-house Five-Paw Chef. Lucky for you, it's easy because it turns out dogs aren't all that picky and don't care what they eat!

Ingredients

  • Bone marrow, frozen (60 pieces)
  • Steak, any cut, raw (4oz per guest)

Steps

Make sure to cook for your flock three times a day to keep them happy and healthy. You may need to hire some help to clean up any presents left behind throughout the day.

  1. Provide one piece of bone marrow every time your pups do something extra cute
  2. Cut up the raw steak into equal portions for each dog
  3. Deliver on a silver platter
  4. Repeat three times a day or mealtime per your feeding schedule
